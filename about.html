About Sunny Cafe

Sunny Cafe was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to bring the warmth of the sun into every cup and bite. Nestled in the heart of the city, our cozy spot has become a favorite for locals and visitors alike.

We source our beans from sustainable farms and bake fresh daily. Our team is passionate about creating a welcoming atmosphere where you can relax and enjoy.
